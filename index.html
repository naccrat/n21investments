Real Estate Investments

N21 Investments is a real estate investment company with a focus on fix and flip properties in Utah and Florida. With over three years of experience in the industry, N21 Investments has established a reputation for acquiring and rehabilitating distressed properties to improve their value and appeal.

The company's mission is to provide high-quality, affordable homes to communities in Utah and Florida while also generating attractive returns for investors.

01.

We started investing in 2019 with single family homes.

Single family homes are a popular choice for many investors because they offer relatively low barriers to entry, as well as the potential for rental income and long-term appreciation.
